Alpbrothers Mountainbike Guiding

Interested in a maximum dirt after-work-ride, a no-pain-no-gain enduro tour or a relaxed day trip in the tri-state area of Austria, Italy and Slovenia with an officially-certified mountain bike guide? We offer individual tours for beginners and the more advanced near Lake Faak / Villach. Reach out to us and join us discovering Carinthia (Kärnten) and the broader area of Lake Faak (Faaker See).

Tours

Technique Training

In our technique training sessions we mainly work on your biking skills. Basic hints on your riding position, braking technique and cornering will help you to keep the flow on every trail, and achieve your ideal setup for off-road riding. Afterwards you can demonstrate your abilities on one of the various exciting trails the beautiful Lake Faak area offers.

Guided Tour

Whether you are interested in a maximum dirt after-work-ride, a no-pain-no-gain enduro tour or a relaxed day trip. We offer all kinds of individual tours in the tri-state area of Austria, Italy and Slovenia. Just send us your inquiry and enjoy your unique experience in the epic scenery of Lake Faak.
