Experience Faaker See

For us, mountain biking is more than just exercise, it is an attitude to life! This unique lifestyle combines endurance, focus, friendship, emotions, culture, and even regional cuisine with the urge to explore nature and to enjoy a breathtaking countryside. Sounds interesting? Tours

Bike Technique Training

In our technique training sessions we mainly work on your biking skills. Basic hints on your riding position, braking technique and cornering will help you to keep the flow on every trail, and achieve your ideal setup for off-road riding. Afterwards you can demonstrate your abilities on one of the various exciting trails the beautiful Faaker See area offers.

Guided Tour

Whether you are interested in a maximum dirt after-work-ride, a no-pain-no-gain enduro tour or a relaxed day trip. We offer all kinds of individual tours in the tri-state area of Austria, Italy and Slovenia. Just send us your inquiry and enjoy your unique experience in the epic scenery of Faaker See.
